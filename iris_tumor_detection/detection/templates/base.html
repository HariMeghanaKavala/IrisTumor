A Better Vision is Our Responsibility

Welcome to the "Iris Tumor Detector"

  • Harnessing the power of advanced machine learning, this tool is designed to analyze eye images and detect potential iris tumors with precision.

  • Our goal is to empower healthcare professionals and patients with accessible, reliable technology for early diagnosis, contributing to better eye health and timely medical interventions.


Steps To Use

  1. Register and Login: Go to the Register page to create an account by providing a valid details.
  2. After logging in, navigate to the Home page.
  3. Click on the Upload Image button to select an eye image from your device.
  4. Ensure the image is clear and follows the format specified (e.g., .jpg, .png, etc.).
  5. After uploading, click the "Detect Tumor" button.
  6. The system will analyze the image and display whether a tumor is detected or not.
  7. Once done, click the Log Out button to safely exit your account.
